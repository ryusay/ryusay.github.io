Thomas Ballatore

Thomas is the Founder and CEO of Ryusay. He has a Ph.D. from University of Tokyo and is a Teaching Fellow of Computer Science at Harvard University. He has been in Japan 24 years and lives near Kyoto with his wife and three children.

Colton Ogden

Colton is Co-Founder and Chief Technical Officer of Ryusay. He is an Instructor of Game Design at Harvard University and a game aficionado. In his spare time, he is a DJ, once ranking 21st in the world at Guitar Hero — rock on!

Keigo Kuroda

Keigo is Co-Founder and Chief Financial Officer of Ryusay. He has known Thomas since his study abroad days at University of Illinois (1993-1994). He has an MBA from Cornell (2000) and was a Director at Blackstone. He is currently the CEO of Crosspath Advisors, based in Tokyo.
